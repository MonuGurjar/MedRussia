The medium of instruction in all universities partnered with MedGuide Russia is strictly 100% English for international students throughout the entire 6-year duration of the MBBS program. However, basic Russian language is taught as a subject in the early years to help you communicate with patients during clinical rotations.

Tuition fees are paid directly to the university's official bank account. You can pay via international wire transfer (SWIFT) or through specialized payment gateways integrated into our student portal.
